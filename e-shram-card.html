Official .gov.in Source

Accident Cover

₹2 Lakh

Who Qualifies

Unorganized Workers (16-59)

Ministry

Labour & Employment

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be an unorganized worker.
  • Age between 16 to 59 years.
  • Should not be a member of EPFO/ESIC/NPS.
  • Should not be an income tax payer.

Benefits of e-Shram Card

  • ₹2 Lakh Accidental Death & Permanent Disability Cover: Under Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY).
  • ₹1 Lakh Partial Disability Cover: Financial assistance in case of partial disability.
  • Access to Social Security Schemes: Facilitates direct benefit transfer for various government schemes.
  • National Database: Helps government in policy-making and direct support for unorganized workers.
  • Portability: Benefits are portable across different states.

How to Apply for e-Shram Card

  1. Visit the Official Portal: Go to the official e-Shram portal.
  2. Click on Registration: Select the "Register on e-Shram" option.
  3. Enter Details & OTP: Provide your Aadhaar-linked mobile number, captcha, and verify with the received OTP.
  4. Fill Application Form: Complete the application form with personal, address, educational, occupational, and bank details.
  5. Review & Submit: Carefully review all entered information and submit the application.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the e-Shram Card?

The e-Shram Card is a 12-digit unique number issued to unorganized workers in India. It is part of a national database created by the Ministry of Labour & Employment to provide social security benefits and support to this segment of the workforce.

Who is considered an unorganized worker?

An unorganized worker is someone who is self-employed, a home-based worker, a wage worker, or an employer in an establishment that employs less than ten workers. This includes construction workers, migrant workers, street vendors, domestic workers, agricultural laborers, etc.

Is there any fee for e-Shram Card registration?

No, registration for the e-Shram Card is completely free of charge. Workers can register themselves through the official portal or visit a Common Service Centre (CSC) for assistance, which may charge a nominal fee for their service.

What if my mobile number is not linked to Aadhaar?

If your mobile number is not linked to your Aadhaar, you will need to visit the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) for e-Shram registration. They will assist you with the biometric authentication process.
