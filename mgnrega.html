👷 What Is MGNREGA

What Makes MGNREGA Unique — A Law, Not a Scheme

The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA) was enacted in 2005 and launched nationally in 2006. It is the world's largest employment guarantee programme, guaranteeing at least 100 days of wage employment per financial year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.

What makes MGNREGA extraordinary is its legal character. It is demand-driven — the government must provide work when you ask for it. If they don't, they must pay you an unemployment allowance. No other welfare programme in the world operates this way at this scale.

💳 Job Card

How to Get Your Free MGNREGA Job Card — In 15 Days

A job card is your MGNREGA identity. Without it, you cannot legally demand work. Every eligible rural household must have one — and it must be issued free.

  1. 1
    Write Application to Gram PanchayatWrite a simple letter listing all adult members (18+) in your household willing to do unskilled work. Include each person's name, age, Aadhaar number, and relationship to head of family.
  2. 2
    Attach Aadhaar Copies and PhotographAttach photocopies of Aadhaar cards for all members and a recent passport-size photo of the head of family. No other documents needed.
  3. 3
    Get Dated ReceiptInsist on a dated receipt from the Gram Panchayat. This proves when you applied — important if card is delayed beyond 15 days.
  4. 4
    Card Issued Within 15 DaysThe Panchayat must issue your job card within 15 days. It contains photos of enrolled members and a unique job card number. Valid for 5 years, renewable.
  5. 5
    Link Bank AccountLink your bank or post office account to the job card. All wages are paid via DBT — Jan Dhan accounts work perfectly.
⚠️ Zero Fee — Report Anyone Charging: Job cards are completely free. No Sarpanch, Panchayat Secretary, or government official can charge you. Call MGNREGA helpline 1800-111-555 immediately if someone demands payment.
📋 Demand Work

How to Demand Work and Get Unemployment Allowance

Formally Demand Work — The Legal Process

  1. 1
    Submit Written Work Demand to Gram PanchayatWrite a simple application stating: date from which you want work, number of days (minimum 14 continuous days), and names of household members who want to work. Give at least 15 days advance notice.
  2. 2
    Obtain Dated AcknowledgementThe Panchayat must give you a dated receipt. This timestamp starts your 15-day legal clock. Without this receipt, proving your demand date becomes difficult.
  3. 3
    Work Must Start Within 15 DaysIf no work is provided within 15 days of your demand date, you are automatically entitled to unemployment allowance — no further application needed.

👩 Women's Special Rights Under MGNREGAAt least 1/3 of all MGNREGA workers must be women. Women get completely equal wages. Women can demand work independently — no husband's permission needed. Creche facilities must be provided at worksites if children under 6 are present. Women cannot be assigned dangerous tasks.
🏗️ Work Types

Types of Work Done Under MGNREGA — Building Rural Infrastructure

MGNREGA work focuses on building durable rural assets that benefit the entire community while providing income to workers. All work is unskilled manual labour:

  • Water conservation: Ponds, check dams, recharge pits, desilting, farm bunds
  • Rural roads: Farm-to-market paths, road repair, culverts
  • Individual assets on SC/ST/BPL land: Private wells, irrigation channels, land levelling
  • Plantation: Social forestry, horticulture, roadside tree planting
  • Flood protection: Embankments, drainage channels, landslide prevention
  • Government building construction: Schools, anganwadis, Panchayat bhawans
  • Sanitation: Individual household toilets under Swachh Bharat Mission
  • Coastal protection: Mangrove plantation, seawall repair in coastal areas
