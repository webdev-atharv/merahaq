👷 Overview

What Is MGNREGA — And Why It Is a Legal Guarantee, Not a Scheme

MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act) is a landmark Indian law enacted in 2005 and launched nationally in 2006. Unlike most welfare schemes, MGNREGA is a legal entitlement — the government is legally obligated to provide you work within 15 days of you demanding it. If they fail, they must pay you unemployment allowance. This is not charity — it is your right.

MGNREGA guarantees at least 100 days of wage employment per financial year to every rural household whose adult members are willing to do unskilled manual work. Wages are paid directly to your bank or post office account — no middlemen, no contractor, no cash handling. Over 15 crore households have used MGNREGA since its launch.

What Makes MGNREGA Unique

  • Demand-driven: The government must give you work when you ask — you don't wait for a project to start
  • Work near home: Employment is provided within 5 km of your residence (10% extra wage if farther)
  • Women's quota: At least one-third of workers must be women, at equal wages
  • Fast payment: Wages must be paid within 15 working days of work completion — delay attracts compensation
  • No contractor: All work is done under direct Gram Panchayat supervision — no private contractor allowed
  • Social audit: Citizens can publicly audit MGNREGA records — a powerful anti-corruption mechanism

💳 Job Card

How to Get Your MGNREGA Job Card — Free & Issued in 15 Days

A Job Card is the gateway to MGNREGA. Without it, you cannot demand work. Every eligible household must get one.

  1. 1
    Submit Application at Gram PanchayatWrite a simple application listing all adult members (18+) in your household willing to do unskilled work. Include each person's name, age, and Aadhaar number.
  2. 2
    Submit with Photograph & AadhaarAttach a recent passport-size photograph of the head of family and photocopies of all members' Aadhaar cards. No fees required.
  3. 3
    Panchayat Issues Card Within 15 DaysThe Gram Panchayat must issue your job card within 15 days. It contains photos of all enrolled members and is valid for 5 years (renewable).
  4. 4
    Link Bank AccountLink your bank or post office account to the job card. All MGNREGA wages are paid through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) — including Jan Dhan accounts.
⚠️ Job Card Must Be Free: No Gram Panchayat official or sarpanch can charge you for issuing a job card. If anyone demands money, report them to the District Programme Coordinator or call the MGNREGA helpline 1800-111-555 immediately.
🚧 Demand Work

How to Demand Work and Get Unemployment Allowance

Demanding Work (Your Legal Right)

  1. 1
    Submit Written Work DemandGive a written application to your Gram Panchayat specifying the date from which you want work, number of days, and names of household members who want to work. Give at least 15 days' advance notice.
  2. 2
    Receive a Dated ReceiptThe Panchayat must give you a dated receipt for your demand. Keep this safe — it is your legal proof of demand if work is not given.
  3. 3
    Work Must Start Within 15 DaysThe Panchayat is legally required to start providing work within 15 days of your demand date. Work must be within 5 km of your home.

Unemployment Allowance (If Work Is Not Given)

If the Gram Panchayat fails to provide work within 15 days of a valid demand, you are legally entitled to an unemployment allowance paid by the state government:

Period Without WorkAllowance Rate
First 30 days of non-provisionAt least 25% of wage rate per day
