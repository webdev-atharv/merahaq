🌿 Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Official .gov.in Source

Quick Facts

  • Benefit: Comprehensive crop insurance against non-preventable natural risks.
  • Who Qualifies: All farmers, including sharecroppers and tenant farmers, cultivating notified crops in notified areas.
  • Ministry: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Government of India.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Farmers cultivating notified crops in notified areas are eligible.
  • Both loanee and non-loanee farmers can avail the scheme.
  • Scheme is compulsory for farmers availing Crop Loan/Kisan Credit Card (KCC) for notified crops.
  • Voluntary for other farmers.

Benefits of PMFBY

The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana offers several key benefits to farmers:

  • Provides financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage arising out of unforeseen events.
  • Stabilizes the income of farmers to ensure their continuance in farming.
  • Encourages farmers to adopt innovative and modern agricultural practices.
  • Ensures flow of credit to the agriculture sector, contributing to food security, crop diversification, and enhancing growth and competitiveness of agriculture sector.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official PMFBY portal: pmfby.gov.in
  2. Register as a new user or log in if you already have an account.
  3. Fill in the application form with all required personal, land, and crop details.
  4. Upload scanned copies of necessary documents (listed below).
  5. Review your application and submit it, then pay the applicable premium online.

Required Documents

  • Application Form (duly filled)
  • ID Proof (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving License)
  • Address Proof (Aadhar Card, Ration Card, Driving License, Utility Bill)
  • Land Records (Khasra, Khatauni, B1, etc.)
  • Sowing Certificate/Proof of Sowing (from Patwari/Village Head)
  • Bank Account Passbook (for direct benefit transfer)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)?

PMFBY is a government-sponsored crop insurance scheme that integrates multiple stakeholders on a single platform. It aims to provide comprehensive insurance coverage to farmers against crop losses due to non-preventable natural risks.

What types of risks are covered under PMFBY?

The scheme covers yield losses due to non-preventable risks, e.g., natural fire and lightning, storm, hailstorm, cyclone, typhoon, tempest, hurricane, tornado, flood, inundation, landslide, drought, dry spells, pests, and diseases.

What are the premium rates for different crops?

Uniform premium rates are 2% for all Kharif crops, 1.5% for all Rabi crops, and 5% for annual commercial and horticultural crops. The remaining premium is borne by the Government.

How can I check my PMFBY application status?

You can check your application status by visiting the official PMFBY portal (pmfby.gov.in) and using your application ID.
