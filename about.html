MeraHaq is a free, independent platform built to help every Indian citizen find government schemes they qualify for, understand their legal rights, and access the tools they need — without any fees, registration, or complexity.

Why MeraHaq Exists

India has hundreds of central and state government welfare schemes — PM Kisan, Ayushman Bharat, MGNREGA, scholarships, housing schemes, insurance schemes, and many more. Billions of rupees are allocated every year for the benefit of ordinary citizens.

Yet most eligible families never claim these benefits. Not because they don't qualify — but because they don't know these schemes exist, don't understand how to apply, or find the process too confusing.

The same is true for legal rights. Every Indian has constitutional and legal rights — the right to free legal aid, the right to file an RTI, the right to emergency hospital treatment, the right against workplace harassment. Most people never exercise these rights simply because nobody told them they had them.

MeraHaq was built to fix this. We present official government information in plain, simple language — so every Indian, regardless of education level or income, can understand what they are entitled to and how to get it.

Important Disclaimer

MeraHaq is an informational tool only. We are not a government website and are not affiliated with any government body, political party, or commercial organisation.

All information on this site is sourced from official Government of India portals including MyScheme.gov.in, pmjay.gov.in, nrega.nic.in, nalsa.gov.in, rtionline.gov.in, uidai.gov.in and individual Ministry websites. While we make every effort to keep information accurate and up to date, government policies and scheme details can change — always verify on official portals before applying.

This site does not provide legal advice. If you need legal help, please contact NALSA at 15100 (free, 24/7) or visit your nearest District Legal Services Authority.

⚠️ Beware of fraud: MeraHaq never asks you to pay any money. No government scheme requires a fee to any private website. If anyone charges you to "apply through MeraHaq," they are scamming you. Report fraud at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
