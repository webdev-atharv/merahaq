## What is MeraHaq?

MeraHaq (मेरा हक़ — meaning "by right") is a free, plain-language guide to government schemes, citizen rights, and entitlements for every Indian. Most Indians are entitled to dozens of government benefits but never claim them because no one explains them clearly.

MeraHaq fixes that.
