What is Voter ID?

The Voter ID card — officially called the Electors Photo Identity Card (EPIC) — is issued by the Election Commission of India to every eligible Indian citizen aged 18 and above. It serves two purposes: it is your proof of voter registration, and it is a widely accepted identity proof across India.

Your Voter ID allows you to vote in Lok Sabha, Rajya Sabha, Vidhan Sabha, and local body elections. Even if you have an Aadhaar card, you still need a separate Voter ID to vote — Aadhaar alone is not accepted at polling booths as the only proof of voter registration.

The digital version is called e-EPIC and can be downloaded free from the official Voter Helpline portal. It is legally valid everywhere that a physical Voter ID is accepted.

Who Can Apply for Voter ID?

  • Any Indian citizen aged 18 years or above as on January 1st of the qualifying year
  • Ordinary resident of the constituency where you want to register
  • Not disqualified under any law (e.g. conviction for certain offences)
  • NRIs (Non-Resident Indians) can also register as overseas voters — a separate category

✅ Turning 18 soon? You can apply for voter registration up to 3 months before your 18th birthday under the new rules introduced in 2023. There are now 4 qualifying dates per year: January 1, April 1, July 1, and October 1.

How to Apply for New Voter ID Online

  1. Go to voters.eci.gov.in
    This is the official Election Commission of India Voter Helpline portal. Do not use any other website.
  2. Click "Register as New Voter" — Form 6
    Fill in your personal details: name, date of birth, address, gender, and mobile number.
  3. Upload documents
    Age proof: Aadhaar, birth certificate, school certificate, or passport. Address proof: Aadhaar, utility bill, bank passbook, or rent agreement. Passport photo.
  4. Submit the form
    You will get a reference number to track your application status.
  5. BLO verification
    A Booth Level Officer (BLO) will visit your address to verify your details. Be available at home.
  6. Card delivery
    After verification, your Voter ID is dispatched to your address. Process takes 4-6 weeks. You can download e-EPIC immediately after approval.

How to Download e-EPIC (Digital Voter ID — Free)

e-EPIC is a digital version of your Voter ID card in PDF format. It is free to download and legally valid everywhere.

  1. Go to voters.eci.gov.in
    Click on "Download e-EPIC" on the homepage.
  2. Enter your EPIC number or mobile number
    Your EPIC number is the 10-character alphanumeric code on your Voter ID card (e.g. ABC1234567).
  3. Verify with OTP
    OTP is sent to your registered mobile number. If mobile is not registered, you will need to visit your Electoral Registration Officer to update it.
  4. Download the PDF
    Save it to your phone or print it. This e-EPIC is accepted at polling booths and as identity proof.

✅ Cannot find your EPIC number? Search your name on the electoral roll at voters.eci.gov.in → "Search in Electoral Roll" — enter your name, father's name, and district to find your voter details.

How to Correct Errors in Voter ID

If your name, address, photo, or date of birth is wrong on your Voter ID, you can correct it by submitting Form 8 online.

  1. Go to voters.eci.gov.in
    Click "Correction of entries in Electoral Roll" — Form 8.
  2. Select what needs correction
    Name spelling, date of birth, photo, address, or other details.
  3. Upload supporting document
    For name: PAN, Aadhaar, or passport. For DOB: birth certificate or school leaving certificate.
  4. Submit and track
    Use your reference number to track status. Correction takes 4-6 weeks.

How to Transfer Voter ID After Moving

If you move to a new address in a different constituency, you need to transfer your voter registration. Submit Form 8A online at voters.eci.gov.in. You will need to provide your new address proof and your existing EPIC number. Your name will be deleted from the old constituency and added to the new one.

If you move within the same constituency, submit Form 8 to update your address without changing your constituency.

⚠️ Do not have two voter registrations. Being registered in two places is illegal. When you transfer, your old registration is automatically deleted.
