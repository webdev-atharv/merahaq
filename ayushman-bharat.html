🏥 How It Works

How PM-JAY Works — Zero Payment at Admission

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY) was launched in September 2018 under the National Health Protection Mission. It is the world's largest government-funded health insurance programme, covering over 55 crore individuals from India's most economically vulnerable families.

PM-JAY works like a cashless insurance card — but the government pays all premiums for you. When hospitalisation is needed at an empanelled hospital: show your Ayushman golden card at the Ayushman Mitra desk → identity verified against the database → treatment authorised → you receive full treatment without paying a single rupee → hospital bills the government directly after discharge.

What "Cashless From Day One" Actually Means

  • No admission deposit — hospitals cannot ask for any advance or security deposit
  • No pre-approval waiting — emergency treatment starts immediately; approval can follow
  • No co-payment — you pay nothing, not even a token amount
  • No claim forms to fill — hospital manages all paperwork directly with the government
  • Medicines and tests included — all medicines, diagnostics, and consumables during hospitalisation are covered
  • Follow-up covered — 3 days pre-hospitalisation + 15 days post-discharge expenses included
🗺️ Portable Across All IndiaYour Ayushman card works in any empanelled hospital in any Indian state. A migrant worker from Bihar working in Surat can get treatment at a Surat empanelled hospital using their Bihar-registered card. Just carry your Aadhaar card and card number.
✅ Eligibility

Who Is Eligible — SECC 2011 Criteria in Detail

Eligibility is determined by the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 database. You do not "apply" for eligibility — your name is either in the database or not. Check at pmjay.gov.in or call 14555.

💳 Get Card

How to Get Your Ayushman Golden Card — Free, Same Day

  1. 1
    Check Eligibility at pmjay.gov.inClick "Am I Eligible" → Enter mobile → OTP → Search by name or ration card number. Confirm your PM-JAY family ID is shown.
  2. 2
    Visit CSC, Government Hospital or Jan Seva KendraCarry your Aadhaar card and ration card. The Ayushman Mitra does biometric verification and prints your golden card — same day, completely free.
  3. 3
    Or Download OnlineVisit beneficiary.nha.gov.in → Login with Aadhaar OTP → Download e-Ayushman card as PDF → Works at all hospitals, no physical card needed.
  4. 4
    Or Use the AppDownload "Ayushman Bharat" app → Login with Aadhaar → Check eligibility and download cards for all family members. Available on Android and iOS.
📌 No Cap on Family Size: All members of an eligible family — parents, children, spouse, siblings — are covered under the same ₹5 lakh annual limit. No limit on number of family members. The ₹5 lakh limit resets every year on your coverage anniversary.
❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — Verified 2026

  • Both government and private hospitals can be empanelled under PM-JAY. Over 13,000 private hospitals are on the empanelled list. Always verify the hospital is currently empanelled at pmjay.gov.in → "Find Hospital" before going for planned treatment. For emergencies, ask the hospital's Ayushman Mitra desk at admission.
  • The ₹5 lakh annual limit applies per family. If treatment costs exceed this, you pay the remaining amount. However, many states have additional health schemes on top of PM-JAY — Andhra Pradesh (AAROGYASRI), Tamil Nadu (CMCHIS), Karnataka (AROGYA BHAGYA) — that extend coverage. Check your state health department for top-up schemes.
  • Empanelled hospitals cannot charge beneficiaries anything. Call 14555 immediately — available 24x7. File a grievance at pmjay.gov.in → "Grievances". Contact the State Health Agency (SHA). Hospitals face suspension from the scheme for charging patients. If denied emergency treatment, it is also a violation of the Clinical Establishments Act.
  • Many states have extended PM-JAY coverage beyond SECC to include additional populations. Check your state Health Department or State Health Agency website for state-specific expansion. For example, J&K's AB-PMJAY SEHAT covers all residents. Some states cover all BPL ration card holders even if not in SECC. Additionally, you can verify through your state's SECC records whether your household was missed during enumeration.
  • Yes. Dialysis is one of PM-JAY's most important covered benefits. All dialysis sessions during a hospitalisation are covered. Additionally, under the PM National Dialysis Programme, free dialysis is available at district hospitals even for patients who may not have the Ayushman card. Ask your district hospital about the National Dialysis Programme specifically.
