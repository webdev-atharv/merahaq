Agnipath Scheme 🪖 Empowering Youth, Securing the Nation

Official .gov.in Source

Quick Facts

₹30,000/month

Starting Salary

17.5 - 21 Years

Age Eligibility

Ministry of Defence

Administering Ministry

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Limit: Candidates must be between 17.5 years and 21 years of age.
  • Educational Qualification: Generally, Class 10th/12th pass from a recognized board. Specific requirements may vary based on the service (Army, Navy, Air Force).
  • Medical Standards: Candidates must meet the prescribed medical standards for enrolment in the Indian Armed Forces.
  • Physical Standards: Must meet the physical standards including height, weight, and chest measurements as per service requirements.
  • Nationality: Indian Citizen.

Benefits of the Agnipath Scheme

The Agnipath Scheme offers a unique opportunity for Indian youth to serve in the Armed Forces for a short duration, providing a range of benefits:

  • Attractive Monthly Emoluments: Starting at ₹30,000 per month in the first year, increasing to ₹40,000 by the fourth year.
  • "Seva Nidhi" Package: A one-time corpus fund of approximately ₹11.71 lakhs (including interest) will be provided to Agniveers upon completion of their four-year tenure, tax-free.
  • Risk & Hardship Allowance: Agniveers will receive risk and hardship allowances as applicable to regular soldiers.
  • Leave: 30 days of annual leave and sick leave based on medical advice.
  • Insurance: Non-contributory Life Insurance Cover of ₹48 lakhs.
  • Skill Development: Enhanced skill sets and experience gained during service, making them highly employable in various sectors.
  • Priority in other jobs: Preference for Agniveers in various government jobs (e.g., CAPFs, Assam Rifles, State Police forces) after completion of their tenure.

How to Apply

  1. Visit Official Recruitment Portals: Access the official websites for Indian Army (joinindianarmy.nic.in), Indian Navy (joinindiannavy.gov.in), or Indian Air Force (agnipathvayu.cdac.in).
  2. Registration: Register yourself by providing basic details and creating an account.
  3. Fill Application Form: Complete the online application form with accurate personal, educational, and other required details.
  4. Upload Documents: Upload scanned copies of necessary documents (educational certificates, photographs, signature, etc.) as specified.
  5. Pay Application Fee: If applicable, pay the required application fee online.
  6. Submit and Print: Review your application, submit it, and take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.

Official Portal: Agnipath Vayu Official Website (for Air Force, similar portals for Army/Navy)

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the tenure of service under the Agnipath Scheme?
The tenure of service for Agniveers is four years, including the training period.
Will Agniveers get permanent absorption in the Armed Forces?
Up to 25% of Agniveers from each batch will be enrolled into the regular cadre of the Armed Forces based on their performance and organizational requirements.
What is the "Seva Nidhi" package?
The "Seva Nidhi" package is a one-time lump sum amount of approximately ₹11.71 lakhs (including interest) that Agniveers will receive upon completion of their four-year service. This amount is tax-free.
Are there any age relaxations for the Agnipath Scheme?
For the recruitment year 2022-23, the upper age limit was relaxed to 23 years. Subsequent recruitments typically adhere to the 17.5 to 21 years age limit, unless specific relaxations are announced by the government.
