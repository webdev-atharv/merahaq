Frequently Asked Questions

Is MeraHaq affiliated with the Government of India?

No. MeraHaq is an independent, free public service website. We are not affiliated with any government body, ministry, or political party. All information is sourced from official .gov.in portals and presented in plain language for easier understanding.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes. MeraHaq does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The scheme finder tool works entirely in your browser. No information you enter is ever sent to our servers. We have no user accounts, no tracking, and no data collection of any kind.

How do I report incorrect information?

Government scheme details change frequently. If you find outdated or incorrect information, please use the contact form above to let us know. We update our content regularly from official government portals. Your feedback helps us keep information accurate and useful for every Indian.

Can I suggest a new scheme or topic to cover?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new government schemes, rights, or topics that would help Indian citizens. Use the contact form to share your suggestion. We read every message and try to add the most requested topics as quickly as possible.

Is MeraHaq free to use?

Yes, completely free. MeraHaq will always be free for every Indian citizen. There are no premium features, no subscriptions, and no hidden charges. Our mission is to make government information accessible to every Indian regardless of their income or education level.
