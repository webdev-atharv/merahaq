🌾 Overview

What Is PM-KISAN — And How ₹6,000 Reaches Your Bank

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) was launched in February 2019 to provide direct income support to all landholding farmer families in India. The scheme delivers ₹6,000 per year in three equal installments of ₹2,000 every four months — deposited directly into the farmer's bank account through DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) with zero middlemen.

Since launch, over ₹3.24 lakh crore has been transferred in 17+ installments to over 11 crore farmers. The money is unconditional — you can use it for seeds, fertilisers, tools, medicine, or any household need. It arrives three times a year, timed to coincide with the start of agricultural seasons when input costs are highest.

Why PM-KISAN Matters — The Real Impact

India's 86% of farmers are small and marginal — owning less than 2 hectares. They routinely borrow from moneylenders at 24–48% annual interest to buy seeds and fertilisers before each season. PM-KISAN's ₹2,000 arrives at season-start, reducing dependence on this debt trap. Over five years, a single registered farmer receives ₹30,000 directly — small individually, transformative at scale.

💡 PM-KISAN + Kisan Credit Card (KCC) = Maximum Benefit If you receive PM-KISAN, you are specifically targeted for KCC (Kisan Credit Card) which gives farm loans at just 4% interest. Use your PM-KISAN installments to repay KCC dues — this reduces your interest burden and maintains your credit score. Visit your bank branch with your PM-KISAN registration number to apply for KCC.
✅ Eligibility

Who Is Eligible — And the Complete Exclusion List

Who Is Eligible

  • All landholding farmer families — families that own cultivable agricultural land in their name as per land revenue records
  • Both small/marginal farmers AND large landholders — there is no upper land ceiling under PM-KISAN
  • Farmers whose land is in the name of any family member — husband, wife, or minor children
  • Farmers in both rural and urban areas where cultivable land records exist

Complete Exclusion List — Who Is NOT Eligible

Excluded CategoryWhy Excluded
Institutional landholdersCompanies, trusts, cooperative societies owning farmland are excluded
Constitutional post holdersCurrent/former President, VP, PM, Governor, Ministers, MPs, MLAs, Mayors
Government employees (salary ₹10,000+/month pension)Regular employees and pensioners above threshold — Multi-tasking/Group D staff exempted
Income tax payeesAnyone who filed income tax return in the last assessment year
Registered professionalsDoctors, engineers, lawyers, CAs, architects in active practice
Tenant farmers / sharecroppersOnly land-owning farmers covered — not those who cultivate leased land
⚠️ If You Became Ineligible After Registration: If your circumstances changed (got a government job, started paying income tax) and you are still receiving PM-KISAN, you must voluntarily surrender the benefit at pmkisan.gov.in → "Voluntary Surrender". Continuing to receive after ineligibility can result in recovery of all past payments with penalty.

How to Check If Installment Was Credited

  • pmkisan.gov.in → Farmer's Corner → "Beneficiary Status" → Enter Aadhaar/mobile/account number
  • PM-KISAN mobile app (Android) → Beneficiary Status
  • PFMS portal (pfms.nic.in) → Know Your Payment → Enter bank account and IFSC
  • Bank passbook — credit appears as "PM-KISAN" with the exact installment date
  • Helpline 155261 — toll-free, 24x7 status inquiry
🔑 eKYC — Mandatory

Complete eKYC in 2 Minutes — 3 Methods Explained

eKYC is mandatory for all PM-KISAN beneficiaries. Without it, your installment is blocked even if you were receiving it for years. Here are all three ways to complete it:

MethodHowCostTime
OTP-based (online)pmkisan.gov.in → eKYC → Aadhaar → OTP on mobileFree2 minutes
Biometric at CSCVisit CSC with Aadhaar card → fingerprint scanFree5 minutes
Face AuthenticationPM-KISAN app → Face eKYC → face scanFree2 minutes
  1. 1
    Visit pmkisan.gov.inGo to the official portal on phone or computer. Click "Farmer's Corner" from the top menu.
  2. 2
    Click "eKYC"Select the eKYC option. Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number in the box provided.
  3. 3
    Enter OTPAn OTP is sent to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. Enter it within 10 minutes. If OTP doesn't come, your mobile number may not be linked to Aadhaar — visit a CSC for biometric eKYC instead.
  4. 4
    Confirmation ScreenYou see a success message: "eKYC done successfully." Your next installment will now be processed automatically.
🖥️ New Registration

How to Register for PM-KISAN — New Farmers Step by Step

  1. 1
    Visit pmkisan.gov.in → New Farmer RegistrationClick "Farmer's Corner" → "New Farmer Registration". Select Rural or Urban category.
  2. 2
    Enter Aadhaar and MobileEnter your 12-digit Aadhaar, state, and Aadhaar-linked mobile. Verify with OTP.
  3. 3
    Fill Personal DetailsEnter name (exactly as on Aadhaar), DOB, category (General/SC/ST/OBC), and bank account details with IFSC code.
  4. 4
    Enter Land DetailsEnter survey number, khata number from your land record (jamabandi/7-12 extract), and land area in hectares.
  5. 5
    Submit and TrackSubmit and save your registration reference number. State verification takes 30–60 days. Once approved, next installment credits automatically.
💡 Documents Needed for RegistrationAadhaar card · Bank account passbook (account number + IFSC) · Land records (survey number, khata number, area) · Active Aadhaar-linked mobile number
