What is PM Kisan?

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi is a central government scheme that gives ₹6,000 per year to all landholding farmer families in India. The money is sent directly to the farmer's bank account in three equal instalments of ₹2,000 each — with no middlemen involved.

The scheme was launched in February 2019 and has since paid over ₹2.5 lakh crore to more than 11 crore farmers across India.

Who Qualifies?

All landholding farmer families are eligible. A "farmer family" means a husband, wife, and minor children who collectively own agricultural land.

❌ Not eligible: Former/current government employees, income tax payers, professionals (doctors, engineers, lawyers, CAs), former/current MPs, MLAs, Ministers, persons with monthly pension above ₹10,000, those who paid income tax in last assessment year.

How to Check Your Payment Status

  1. Go to pmkisan.gov.in
    This is the only official website. Beware of fake sites.
  2. Click "Beneficiary Status"
    Found in the "Farmers Corner" section on the homepage.
  3. Enter your Aadhaar number or mobile number
    Select which type of ID you want to use and enter the number.
  4. Click "Get Data"
    You will see all past payments, their dates, and whether future payments are pending or on hold.

How to Register (New Farmers)

  1. Go to pmkisan.gov.in → "New Farmer Registration"
    Under the Farmers Corner section.
  2. Enter your Aadhaar number
    Select Rural or Urban farmer as applicable.
  3. Fill in your details
    Name, address, bank account number, IFSC code, land records.
  4. Upload land records
    Khasra/Khatauni or land ownership documents.
  5. Submit and note your reference number
    Your application will be verified by the state government. This takes 2–4 weeks.

✅ You can also register at your nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or Gram Panchayat — they will help fill the form for free.

My Payment is Stuck — How to Fix?

If your payment shows "Payment Pending" or "Failed", the most common reasons and fixes are:

  • Wrong bank account number or IFSC → Visit pmkisan.gov.in → "Updation of Self Registered Farmer" and correct the details
  • Aadhaar not linked to bank account → Visit your bank branch and link Aadhaar to your account
  • e-KYC not done → Visit pmkisan.gov.in → "e-KYC" section and complete OTP verification, OR visit nearest CSC
  • Land records mismatch → Visit your Patwari/Tehsildar office to correct land records
  • Name mismatch between Aadhaar and bank → Visit bank or Aadhaar centre to match names

✅ Most payment issues are fixed by completing e-KYC. This is mandatory. Do it at pmkisan.gov.in or at any CSC centre near you.

Documents Required

📄 Aadhaar Card 📄 Bank Passbook / Account details 📄 Land ownership documents (Khasra/Khatauni) 📄 Mobile number linked to Aadhaar
