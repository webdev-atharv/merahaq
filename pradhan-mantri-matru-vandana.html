🤱 PM Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

Financial assistance for pregnant women and lactating mothers.

Official .gov.in Source

Quick Facts

Benefit Amount

₹5,000

Who Qualifies

Pregnant Women & Lactating Mothers

Ministry

Ministry of Women & Child Development

Eligibility Criteria

  • Pregnant Women and Lactating Mothers (PW&LM) for their first live birth.
  • Women who are employed with the Central Government or State Government or Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) are excluded.
  • Women who are already beneficiaries under any other scheme providing similar benefits are excluded.

Benefits of PMMVY

Under the Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana, a cash incentive of ₹5,000 is provided in three installments to eligible beneficiaries:

  • First Installment (₹1,000): On early registration of pregnancy at the Anganwadi Centre (AWC) / approved Health Facility.
  • Second Installment (₹2,000): After at least one antenatal check-up (ANC) and six months of pregnancy.
  • Third Installment (₹2,000): After child birth is registered and the child has received the first cycle of BCG, OPV, DPT and Hepatitis-B or its equivalent/substitute doses.

The cash incentive is directly transferred to the beneficiary's bank/post office account.

How to Apply

  1. Registration: Register at the nearest Anganwadi Centre (AWC) or approved health facility within the first 150 days of pregnancy.
  2. Form Submission: Fill out Form 1A (Registration Form) along with required documents.
  3. Antenatal Check-up: Ensure you undergo at least one antenatal check-up (ANC) to be eligible for the second installment.
  4. Child Birth & Immunization: Register the child's birth and ensure the child receives the first cycle of immunization.
  5. Official Portal: For more details and to download forms, visit the official PMMVY portal: pmmvy.nic.in

Required Documents

  • Mother and Child Protection (MCP) Card
  • Aadhar Card of the beneficiary
  • Bank/Post Office Account Passbook (with IFSC code)
  • Husband's Aadhar Card (optional, for some forms)
  • Declaration/Undertaking Form

Helpline

For any queries or assistance regarding PM Matru Vandana Yojana, you can contact the official helpline:

Toll-Free Number: 1075

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: Is PMMVY applicable for the second child?

A1: No, PMMVY is primarily for the first live birth of a woman. However, recent amendments allow for the second child if it is a girl, to promote the girl child.

Q2: What if I don't have an Aadhar Card?

A2: An Aadhar Card is a mandatory document for PMMVY. You should apply for one immediately if you don't have it.

Q3: How long does it take to receive the installments?

A3: After successful verification of documents and fulfilling the conditions, the installments are typically processed within a few weeks to a month. Delays may occur due to administrative reasons.

Q4: Can government employees avail of this scheme?

A4: No, women who are employed with the Central Government, State Government, or Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) are not eligible for PMMVY.
