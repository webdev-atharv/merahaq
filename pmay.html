🏠 Housing Scheme Guide

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana
Free House for Every Indian

PMAY gives free or subsidised houses to homeless and kutcha-house families across rural and urban India. Crores of families qualify but don't know it.

Two Types of PMAY

🌾 PMAY — GRAMIN (Rural)
For Villages
Administered by Ministry of Rural Development. For families in SECC 2011 list living in kutcha or damaged houses. Grant paid directly to bank account in 3 instalments. Portal: pmayg.nic.in
🏙️ PMAY — URBAN
For Cities & Towns
Administered by Ministry of Housing. For EWS/LIG/MIG urban families who do not own a pucca house. Provides credit-linked subsidy on home loans. Portal: pmaymis.gov.in

Who is Eligible?

PMAY Gramin (Rural):

  • Families listed in SECC 2011 who are houseless or living in 0 or 1 room kutcha house
  • SC/ST families, minorities, freed bonded labourers, widows, disabled — given priority
  • Must not have already received housing assistance from any government scheme

PMAY Urban:

  • EWS: Annual family income up to ₹3 lakh
  • LIG: Annual family income ₹3–6 lakh
  • MIG-I: Annual family income ₹6–12 lakh
  • MIG-II: Annual family income ₹12–18 lakh
  • Family must not own a pucca house anywhere in India
  • For EWS/LIG: House must be in woman's name or co-owned with woman

❌ Not eligible if you or any family member already owns a pucca (permanent) house anywhere in India, or has previously benefited from any central/state government housing scheme.

How to Apply — PMAY Gramin

  1. Check if your name is in the SECC 2011 awaiting list
    Visit pmayg.nic.in → "Stakeholders" → "IAY/PMAYG Beneficiary" → Search by registration number or your state/district/village list.
  2. If your name is present — contact your Gram Panchayat
    The Gram Panchayat selects beneficiaries from the list. Ensure your name hasn't been wrongly skipped.
  3. If your name is missing — apply through Gram Sabha
    Attend your Gram Sabha meeting and request to be added to the waitlist. Bring proof of kutcha house (photos, documents).
  4. Complete Awaas+ Survey
    A government field worker will survey your house. Cooperate fully and ensure all details are correct.
  5. Receive grant in 3 instalments
    ₹40,000 to start construction → ₹60,000 at lintel level → ₹20,000 on completion. Paid directly to your bank account.

How to Apply — PMAY Urban

  1. Go to pmaymis.gov.in
    Click "Citizen Assessment" → select the appropriate category (Slum Dweller / Other 3 Components).
  2. Enter your Aadhaar number
    Your name and details will be auto-filled from Aadhaar.
  3. Fill in the application form
    Provide family income details, bank account, current address, and details about current housing.
  4. Submit and note your Application ID
    Track status at pmaymis.gov.in using your application ID.
  5. For Credit Linked Subsidy (CLSS)
    Apply for a home loan from any bank/HFC. The bank will apply for the subsidy on your behalf. Subsidy is credited to your loan account, reducing EMI.

✅ You can also apply at any Common Service Centre (CSC) or through your Urban Local Body (ULB/Municipality) office. They will assist you free of charge.

Documents Required

📄 Aadhaar Card (all family members) 📄 Bank Account Details 📄 Income Certificate 📄 Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC) 📄 Photo of existing house (kutcha/damaged) 📄 Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM) toilet registration 📄 MGNREGA Job Card (if applicable) 📄 Self-declaration of no pucca house

Track Your Application Status

  • PMAY Gramin: Visit pmayg.nic.in → "Awaassoft" → "Report" → "Beneficiary Details for verification" → enter your registration number
  • PMAY Urban: Visit pmaymis.gov.in → "Citizen Assessment" → "Track Your Assessment Status" → enter application ID or Aadhaar/mobile number
