🔍 Check Your Status

How to Check PM Kisan Payment Status

Follow these steps to check if your installment has been credited:

  • 1
    Go to Official WebsiteOpen pmkisan.gov.in on your phone or computer.
  • 2
    Click "Beneficiary Status"Find it in the top menu under "Farmers Corner".
  • 3
    Enter Your DetailsEnter your Aadhaar number OR registered mobile number OR bank account number.
  • 4
    Click "Get Data"Your full payment history will appear — all installments received and pending.
  • 5
    Check Bank AccountIf status shows "Payment Success" but money not received, check with your bank — it may take 2–3 days to reflect.
📱 Check on Mobile: You can also check status via the PM Kisan mobile app available on Google Play Store. Search "PM Kisan" and install the official app by the Ministry of Agriculture.
⚠️ Money Not Received?

Why Your PM Kisan Installment Did Not Come

These are the most common reasons why farmers do not receive their installment:

  • e-KYC not done: This is the #1 reason. Without e-KYC your payment is blocked. Do it immediately at pmkisan.gov.in — it is free.
  • Aadhaar not linked to bank: Your Aadhaar must be linked to the bank account registered in PM Kisan. Link it at your bank branch or ATM.
  • Wrong bank account details: If IFSC or account number is wrong, payment will fail. Correct it at pmkisan.gov.in under "Updation of Self Registered Farmer".
  • Land records not verified: Your state government must verify your land records. Contact your local patwari or agriculture office.
  • Name mismatch: Name in Aadhaar must match name in PM Kisan records. Get it corrected at your nearest CSC center.
  • Ineligible category: Income taxpayers, government employees, and institutional landholders are not eligible.
⚠️ Do e-KYC Before Next Installment: If your e-KYC is pending, do it RIGHT NOW at pmkisan.gov.in → Farmers Corner → e-KYC. Use OTP on your Aadhaar-linked mobile. Takes 2 minutes. Free.
📲 e-KYC Guide

How to Do PM Kisan e-KYC Online

  • 1
    Go to pmkisan.gov.inOpen the official PM Kisan portal.
  • 2
    Click "e-KYC" in Farmers CornerYou will find it in the top navigation menu.
  • 3
    Enter Your Aadhaar NumberType your 12-digit Aadhaar number and click Search.
  • 4
    Enter OTPAn OTP will be sent to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. Enter it.
  • 5
    e-KYC Done!You will see a success message. Your next installment will now be processed.
📍 No mobile linked to Aadhaar? Visit your nearest CSC (Common Service Centre) or PM Kisan Seva Kendra. They will do biometric e-KYC for you. Charge is usually ₹0–₹30.
