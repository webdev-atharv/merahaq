PAN Card — Complete Guide
for Every Indian

How to apply for a new PAN card, download your e-PAN for free, correct errors, and link PAN to Aadhaar — all explained simply using official government portals.

Issued by Income Tax Department · Required for banking, tax filing & financial transactions

What is PAN Card?

PAN (Permanent Account Number) is a 10-character alphanumeric identifier issued by the Income Tax Department of India to every taxpayer and financial entity. It is mandatory for filing income tax returns, opening bank accounts, buying or selling property above ₹10 lakh, and most financial transactions above ₹50,000.

PAN is not just for people who pay taxes. Even if your income is below the taxable limit, you need PAN to open a bank account, invest in mutual funds, buy insurance above certain limits, and apply for many government services. Every Indian adult should have a PAN card.

How to Apply for New PAN Online

You can apply for PAN through two official portals — NSDL (onlineservices.nsdl.com) or UTIITSL (www.utiitsl.com). Both are official and charge the same fee.

  1. Go to onlineservices.nsdl.com or utiitsl.com
    Click on "Apply Online" under PAN services. Select "New PAN — Indian Citizen (Form 49A)" for Indian residents.
  2. Fill Form 49A online
    Enter your full name (as per Aadhaar), date of birth, father's name, address, email, and mobile number. Select the type of applicant (individual, company, etc.).
  3. Upload documents
    Identity proof: Aadhaar card (preferred). Address proof: Aadhaar, utility bill, bank statement. Date of birth proof: Aadhaar, birth certificate, matriculation certificate.
  4. Pay the fee
    ₹107 for Indian address (includes physical card delivery). ₹1,017 for foreign address. Payment via net banking, debit/credit card, or demand draft.
  5. Note your acknowledgement number
    Use this 15-digit number to track your application at tin.tin.nsdl.com.
  6. Physical PAN delivered in 15-20 days
    Sent by post to your address. e-PAN (digital) is available for download within 2-3 days of approval.

How to Get Free Instant e-PAN (Only for Aadhaar Holders)

If you have an Aadhaar card with a registered mobile number, you can get an e-PAN completely free within minutes through the Income Tax e-filing portal.

  1. Go to incometax.gov.in
    Click "Instant e-PAN" on the homepage — no login required.
  2. Enter your Aadhaar number
    Accept the terms and click "Continue".
  3. Verify with OTP
    OTP is sent to your Aadhaar-registered mobile number.
  4. e-PAN generated instantly
    Download the PDF. Your PAN number is also sent to your email. This e-PAN is free and valid forever.

✅ Instant e-PAN is completely free and valid for all financial transactions. You only need to pay ₹107 if you want a physical plastic card sent to your address.

How to Download e-PAN (Already Have PAN)

If you already have a PAN number but lost your card or need a digital copy, you can download your e-PAN for free.

  1. Go to incometax.gov.in
    Click "Instant e-PAN" → then "Check Status / Download PAN".
  2. Enter Aadhaar number
    Verify with OTP sent to your Aadhaar-registered mobile.
  3. Download e-PAN PDF
    Free to download. Valid for all purposes.

Alternatively, go to onlineservices.nsdl.com → "Download e-PAN" → enter PAN number, Aadhaar, and date of birth → verify OTP → download.

How to Correct Errors in PAN Card

If your name, date of birth, or other details are wrong on your PAN card, you can correct them by applying for a PAN correction/reprint.

  1. Go to onlineservices.nsdl.com or utiitsl.com
    Select "Changes or Correction in PAN Data / Reprint of PAN Card (Form 49A)".
  2. Enter existing PAN number
    Fill in the corrected details and select what needs to be changed.
  3. Upload supporting documents
    Proof for the corrected field — e.g. Aadhaar for name/DOB correction.
  4. Pay ₹107
    Same fee as new PAN application. A corrected physical card is sent within 15-20 days.

⚠️ Name must match exactly with Aadhaar and bank records. Even small spelling differences can cause problems when filing returns or receiving refunds. Correct name mismatches as soon as possible.

How to Link PAN with Aadhaar

PAN-Aadhaar linking is mandatory for all Indian residents who have both. Unlinked PANs are treated as inoperative — you face 2x TDS deductions and cannot file returns.

  1. Go to incometax.gov.in
    Click "Link Aadhaar" on the quick links on the homepage. No login needed.
  2. Enter PAN and Aadhaar number
    Fill in your name as per Aadhaar and your mobile number.
  3. Pay ₹1,000 penalty (if applicable)
    Late linking attracts a fee. Pay via Challan 280 on the NSDL portal first, then proceed with linking.
  4. Verify OTP and submit
    Linking is confirmed in 4-5 working days. Check status at incometax.gov.in → "Link Aadhaar Status".

PAN Card Helpline & Official Links

  • Income Tax Helpline: 1800-103-0025 (free, Mon–Sat 8AM–8PM)
  • NSDL PAN portal: onlineservices.nsdl.com
  • UTIITSL PAN portal: utiitsl.com
  • Instant e-PAN (free): incometax.gov.in
  • PAN application track: tin.tin.nsdl.com

⚠️ Never apply through unofficial agents or websites. There are many fraud websites that charge ₹500-2000 for PAN cards. The official fee is only ₹107. Use only the official portals listed above.
