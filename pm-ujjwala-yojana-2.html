About PM Ujjwala Yojana 2.0

The Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY) 2.0 is a flagship scheme of the Government of India launched in 2016 to provide clean cooking fuel (LPG) to rural and deprived households. Ujjwala 2.0 was launched in August 2021 to further expand the reach of the scheme, providing an additional 1 crore LPG connections to eligible households that could not be covered under the first phase.

The scheme aims to safeguard the health of women and children by providing them with clean cooking fuel, thereby freeing them from the drudgery of collecting firewood and the health hazards of smoke-filled kitchens.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Applicant must be a woman, aged 18 years or above.
  • Applicant must belong to a Below Poverty Line (BPL) household.
  • No other LPG connection should exist in the same household.
  • Applicant must belong to one of the following categories: SC/ST households, PMAY (Gramin), AAY, Most Backward Classes, Tea & Ex-Tea Garden Tribes, Forest Dwellers, Islands & River Islands.
  • Applicants who migrated from their original place of residence can also apply for a connection under Ujjwala 2.0 without a ration card or address proof. A self-declaration of address and family undertaking is sufficient.

Benefits of PM Ujjwala Yojana 2.0

Under Ujjwala 2.0, beneficiaries receive:

  • Financial support of ₹1600 for each LPG connection.
  • First refill and hotplate (stove) free of cost to the beneficiaries.
  • A complete package to ensure a smooth transition to clean cooking fuel.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official PM Ujjwala Yojana portal: pmuy.gov.in
  2. Click on "Apply for New Ujjwala 2.0 Connection" on the homepage.
  3. Select your preferred LPG distributor (Indane, Bharatgas, or HP Gas).
  4. Fill out the online application form with all required details.
  5. Upload scanned copies of the necessary documents.
  6. Submit the application. Your application will be processed by the LPG field official.

Required Documents

  • KYC document (Aadhaar Card is mandatory for proof of identity and proof of address for the applicant).
  • BPL Card / Ration Card / Document from State Government certifying BPL household.
  • Bank Account Number and IFSC Code.
  • Passport size photograph of the applicant.
  • Self-declaration for migrant applicants (for address proof and family undertaking).

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for Ujjwala 2.0?
Women aged 18+ from BPL households who do not have an existing LPG connection and belong to specific categories (SC/ST, PMAY, AAY, etc.). Migrant applicants can also apply with a self-declaration.
What benefits do I get under Ujjwala 2.0?
Beneficiaries receive financial assistance of ₹1600, a free first LPG refill, and a free hotplate (stove).
Is Aadhaar card mandatory for application?
Yes, Aadhaar card is mandatory for identity and address proof. For migrant applicants, a self-declaration can serve as address proof.
Where can I apply for Ujjwala 2.0?
You can apply online through the official PM Ujjwala Yojana portal at pmuy.gov.in or by visiting your nearest LPG distributor.
