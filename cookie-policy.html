Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026  ·  merahaq.online

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are used on MeraHaq, why, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit — like which pages you visited or your preferences. Some cookies are set by the website itself; others are set by third-party services the website uses.

MeraHaq uses a minimal number of cookies, only for analytics and advertising purposes. We do not use cookies for user tracking, login sessions, or personalisation of editorial content.

✅ MeraHaq does not set any first-party tracking cookies. We do not use session cookies, login cookies, shopping cart cookies, or any cookie for personalising editorial content. The only first-party cookie that may appear is from Google Analytics, which we use to understand site traffic in aggregate.

Google Analytics — How We Use It

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand aggregate traffic patterns on MeraHaq — which pages are most useful, how people find the site, and what devices they use. This helps us improve the content and make the site more useful.

Google Analytics data is anonymised — IP addresses are truncated before storage and we cannot identify individual visitors. We only see aggregate reports like "the Ayushman Bharat guide received 1,200 visits this week."

We have enabled IP anonymisation in our Google Analytics setup to further protect your privacy.

Google AdSense — How It Works

MeraHaq uses Google AdSense to display advertisements that help fund the cost of maintaining this free website. Google AdSense may serve personalised ads based on your interests, which are inferred from your browsing history across websites that use Google advertising technologies.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time. When you opt out, you will still see ads but they will be generic rather than interest-based.

How to Control Cookies

You have several options to control how cookies work on your browser:

  • Browser settings: All major browsers allow you to block, delete, or restrict cookies. Look in your browser's Privacy or Security settings.
  • Google Analytics opt-out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on to prevent GA from tracking your visits across all websites.
  • Google Ads opt-out: Visit Google Ads Settings to opt out of interest-based advertising.
  • YourAdChoices: Visit YourAdChoices to manage ad preferences across many networks.
  • NAI Opt-Out: Visit the Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out page to opt out from multiple ad networks at once.

Please note that blocking cookies will not affect the functionality of MeraHaq's content — the Scheme Finder and RTI Tool work entirely in your browser and do not use cookies at all.
