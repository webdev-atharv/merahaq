Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026  ·  merahaq.online

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files saved in your browser when you visit a website. MeraHaq uses cookies only for two purposes — understanding how people use the site (analytics) and showing advertisements (to keep the site free). We do not use cookies to track you personally or store any personal information.

Cookies We Use

📊 Google Analytics Analytics
Helps us see how many people visit MeraHaq and which pages are most helpful — so we can improve the site. All data is completely anonymous. We cannot identify you personally.
📢 Google AdSense Advertising
Shows advertisements that help us keep MeraHaq free. Google may show ads based on your general interests. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time — you will still see ads, just not interest-based ones.
🔤 Google Fonts Functional
Loads the fonts used on this site. Required for the website to display correctly. Does not track you or set advertising cookies.
✅ MeraHaq does not use login cookies, shopping cookies, or any cookie to track you personally. The Scheme Finder and RTI Tool use zero cookies — everything stays in your browser.

How to Control Cookies

You are in full control. Here is how to manage cookies:

  • Use our banner: Click "Reject" on the cookie banner to disable analytics and advertising cookies.
  • Browser settings: Go to your browser's Privacy or Security settings to block or delete cookies at any time.
  • Opt out of Google ads: Visit Google Ads Settings to turn off personalised advertising.
  • Opt out of analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

Blocking cookies will not affect any content on MeraHaq — everything works normally.
