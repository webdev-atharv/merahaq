PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana 🌾

Ensuring food security for the vulnerable sections of society.

Official .gov.in Source

Quick Facts

Benefit Amount

5 kg of free food grains (wheat/rice) per person per month.

Who Qualifies

All beneficiaries covered under the National Food Security Act (NFSA).

Ministry

Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution.

Eligibility

  • Must be a beneficiary under the National Food Security Act (NFSA), 2013.
  • Includes Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) households.
  • Includes Priority Households (PHH).
  • Must possess a valid ration card.

Benefits

Under PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY), eligible beneficiaries receive:

  • 5 kg of free food grains (either rice or wheat) per person per month.
  • This is in addition to the subsidized food grains already provided under the NFSA.
  • The scheme aims to alleviate the financial burden on the poor and vulnerable during challenging times.

How to Apply

PMGKAY benefits are automatically extended to eligible NFSA beneficiaries. No separate application is required.

  1. Ensure you are registered as a beneficiary under the National Food Security Act (NFSA).
  2. Possess a valid ration card (e.g., AAY or PHH).
  3. Visit your designated Fair Price Shop (FPS) or ration shop.
  4. Present your ration card or Aadhaar card for biometric authentication.
  5. Receive your entitled 5 kg of free food grains per person.
  6. For any issues related to your ration card or NFSA eligibility, contact your local Food and Civil Supplies Department or visit the official NFSA Portal.

Required Documents

  • Valid Ration Card (AAY or PHH)
  • Aadhaar Card (for biometric authentication at FPS)

Helpline

For assistance related to food grain distribution or ration card issues, please contact the National Food Security Act (NFSA) helpline:

Toll-Free Number: 1967 or 1800-180-2000

You can also contact your respective State/UT Food & Civil Supplies Department.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PM Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY)?

PMGKAY is a food security welfare scheme announced by the Government of India to provide free food grains to the poor and vulnerable sections of society.

Who is eligible for PMGKAY benefits?

All beneficiaries covered under the National Food Security Act (NFSA), including Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) and Priority Households (PHH), are eligible.

How much food grain do I get under PMGKAY?

Each eligible person receives 5 kg of free food grains (wheat or rice) per month, in addition to their regular NFSA entitlement.

Do I need to apply separately for PMGKAY?

No, there is no separate application process. If you are an NFSA beneficiary with a valid ration card, you are automatically entitled to PMGKAY benefits.
