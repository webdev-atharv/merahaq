📖 Overview

What Is PM Garib Kalyan Yojana — And What Changed in 2023

PM Garib Kalyan Ann Yojana (PMGKAY) was originally launched in March 2020 as a COVID-19 emergency food relief programme — providing additional free grain over and above the subsidised NFSA entitlement. During COVID, it distributed an extra 5 kg per person per month on top of the regular ration, helping families cope with income loss during lockdowns.

In a landmark decision on December 23, 2022, the government announced that from January 1, 2023, the PMGKAY free grain component would be merged into the regular NFSA entitlement. This means all 81.35 crore NFSA cardholders now receive their 5 kg per person per month completely free — no payment, not even the earlier subsidised rate of ₹2–3 per kg for wheat and rice.

The scheme has been extended through December 2028, giving five years of guaranteed free food security to the poorest Indians — the longest such commitment in India's history. The government bears the full cost, estimated at approximately ₹2 lakh crore per year.

💡 Ration Shop Is Collecting Money? That Is Illegal.Since January 2023, all NFSA beneficiaries are entitled to their 5 kg per person completely free. If your ration shop dealer collects any money for grain, they are committing fraud. Report immediately to your state's Food and Civil Supplies department helpline or to the National Consumer Helpline 1800-11-4000.

Who Is NOT Covered and Should Apply for Ration Card

Families without any ration card are excluded from PMGKAY. If your household has no ration card and your annual income is below ₹1 lakh (rural) or ₹1.5 lakh (urban), apply for a new ration card at your state's Food and Civil Supplies office or online via your state government portal. You will be issued a Priority Household card after verification, giving you free grain entitlement.

🔍 How to Check and Claim

How to Check Your Ration Entitlement and Claim It — Step by Step

  1. 1
    Check Your Ration Card Details OnlineVisit the National Food Security Portal at nfsa.gov.in. Enter your ration card number or Aadhaar to see your household's entitlement, transactions, and last pickup record.
  2. 2
    Visit Your Ration Shop (PDS Outlet)Visit your designated Fair Price Shop (ration shop) any time during the month. Bring your ration card or Aadhaar card. The shop has an electronic Point of Sale (ePoS) machine for Aadhaar-based biometric verification.
  3. 3
    Biometric AuthenticationPlace your finger on the biometric machine or use iris scan. The machine verifies your Aadhaar and confirms your entitlement in real-time. Grain is dispensed after successful authentication.
  4. 4
    One Nation One Ration Card (ONORC)If you have migrated to another state, you can collect your ration at any PDS outlet in India using your Aadhaar — regardless of which state issued your ration card. This ONORC facility is active in all 36 states/UTs.
⚠️ Aadhaar Must Be Linked to Ration Card: Without Aadhaar-ration card linking, biometric authentication at the PDS shop won't work. Get your Aadhaar linked at the ration shop itself or at any CSC. This is free and takes 5 minutes.
