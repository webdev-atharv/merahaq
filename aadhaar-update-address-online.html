📄 Accepted Documents

Documents Accepted for Address Update — Complete List

You need just one document from the list below as proof of your new address:

Tier 1 — Strong Address Proof (Preferred)

💻 Passport (valid) 🗳️ Voter ID / EPIC card 🚗 Driving licence 🏦 Bank passbook / statement (last 3 months) 📝 Ration card ⚡ Electricity bill (last 3 months) 📱 Post-paid mobile bill (last 3 months) 💳 Aadhaar of spouse / parent (with relationship proof) 🏠 Property tax receipt (last year) 📜 Water / gas connection bill

If You Have No Documents — Address Validation Letter

If you genuinely don't have any of the above documents at your new address, you can use the Address Validation Letter process. Your Aadhaar holder (verifier) who already has a valid Aadhaar at the same address initiates the process online — and after their OTP verification, your address update goes through without any document upload from your side.

🖥️ Online Process

Step-by-Step Online Address Update at myAadhaar

  1. 1
    Visit myaadhaar.uidai.gov.inGo to the official myAadhaar portal. Click "Update Aadhaar Online" in the services section. Do not use any third-party websites.
  2. 2
    Login with Aadhaar and OTPEnter your 12-digit Aadhaar number and verify with OTP sent to your currently registered mobile number on Aadhaar. This is why your mobile must be linked before updating address online.
  3. 3
    Select "Update Address"From the services list, select "Update Address in Aadhaar". Enter your new complete address — house number, street, village/town, district, state, and PIN code.
  4. 4
    Upload Proof of AddressUpload a clear scan or photograph of your address proof document (file under 2MB, JPEG or PDF). Ensure all text in the document is clearly visible.
  5. 5
    Verify and SubmitReview the entered address carefully. Submit the request. You receive a Service Request Number (SRN) — save this to track your update status.
  6. 6
    Track and Get Updated AadhaarTrack update status at myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in using your SRN. Processing takes 30–90 days. Once approved, download your updated e-Aadhaar from the same portal — free of cost.
⚠️ Mobile Not Registered? Online Won't Work:Online Aadhaar updates require OTP on your registered mobile. If your mobile is not linked or has changed, you must visit an Aadhaar Seva Kendra first to update the mobile number biometrically. After that, you can do address updates online.
❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions — Verified 2025

  • Rejection usually happens because: the uploaded document is not clear, the name on the document doesn't match the Aadhaar name, or the document is too old. Re-submit with a clearer scan of a different accepted document. If the name mismatch is due to spelling, first update your name in Aadhaar, then update the address.
  • A registered rent agreement is accepted. An unregistered rent agreement is not accepted as standalone proof but can be used with a notarized affidavit in some cases. A better option for tenants is a recent bank statement, utility bill in your name at the new address, or the Address Validation Letter process where your landlord verifies your address.
  • Once your update is approved (check status via SRN at myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in), download your updated e-Aadhaar: go to myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in → "Download Aadhaar" → enter Aadhaar number → OTP verification → download PDF. The PDF is password protected — password is the first 4 letters of your name in caps + birth year (e.g., RAJE1990).
