1. Who We Are

MeraHaq ("we", "us", "our") is a free, independent informational website at merahaq.online. We help Indian citizens find government schemes they qualify for, understand their legal rights, access important helplines, and generate RTI letters.

MeraHaq is not affiliated with any government body, political party, or commercial organisation. We are an independent public information service run by a small team of Indian citizens.

Contact: Use our Contact page for any privacy-related questions.

2. Information We Collect

MeraHaq collects minimal information. Here is exactly what is and is not collected:

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or Aadhaar number through any form (other than the optional contact form)
  • Your precise location or GPS coordinates
  • Any information you enter in the Scheme Finder tool — it runs entirely in your browser and is never transmitted
  • Any information you enter in the RTI Letter Generator — it also runs entirely in your browser
  • Device fingerprints or persistent identifiers beyond standard Google Analytics anonymised data

We DO collect (anonymously through Google Analytics):

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General location by city/region (not precise)
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet) and browser type
  • How you arrived at the site (search engine, direct link, etc.)

The Scheme Finder and RTI Letter Tool work 100% in your browser. Nothing you type into these tools is ever sent to MeraHaq servers or stored anywhere.

3. Contact Form Data

If you use the Contact form on our website, the following is sent to us via Telegram (our messaging service):

  • The topic you selected
  • Your name (optional — you can stay completely anonymous)
  • Your message
  • Your contact handle such as a Telegram username (optional — only if you choose to provide it)

This information is used only to respond to your message. We do not share it with any third party, store it in any database, or use it for marketing purposes. Messages are received directly in our Telegram inbox and are not logged on any server.

5. Google AdSense & Advertising

MeraHaq displays advertisements served by Google AdSense to support the cost of maintaining this free website. Here is exactly how this works and what it means for your privacy:

  • Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your visits to MeraHaq and other websites you have previously visited.
  • Google uses the DoubleClick cookie to serve interest-based ads across the web.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • No personally identifiable information (name, email, Aadhaar, phone number) is shared with Google AdSense from MeraHaq, because we do not collect it.

📢 Advertising Transparency: Ads on MeraHaq are clearly labelled "Sponsored" or "Advertisement." We do not accept paid editorial content, sponsored articles, or payment to promote specific schemes or services. All editorial content is independent.

Opt out of personalised advertising:

  • Visit Google Ads Settings to opt out of interest-based ads from Google
  • Visit Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out to opt out from multiple ad networks at once
  • Visit YourAdChoices.com for additional advertising opt-out options

For more information about how Google uses data when you use sites that use Google AdSense, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

6. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use MeraHaq — which pages are most visited, how long people stay, and what devices they use. This helps us improve the site.

Google Analytics data is anonymised — IP addresses are truncated and no personally identifiable information is collected or shared with us. We use this data only in aggregate form (e.g. "500 people visited the Ayushman Bharat page today").

To opt out of Google Analytics across all sites, install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. Third-Party Links

MeraHaq links exclusively to official Government of India websites with .gov.in, .nic.in, or .org.in domains. When you click these links, you leave MeraHaq and are subject to the terms and privacy policies of those external government websites.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or availability of any external website — including official government portals. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any website you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

MeraHaq does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Our website contains only general informational content about government schemes and legal rights — it is suitable for all ages but is not directed at children.

If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information through our contact form, please contact us immediately through our Contact page and we will delete it promptly.

Note that Google AdSense has its own policies regarding personalised advertising to children and users under 18. MeraHaq does not knowingly target advertising to minors.

9. Data Security

MeraHaq takes reasonable measures to protect the limited data we handle:

  • The website is served over HTTPS (SSL encryption) at all times
  • The contact form uses Vercel serverless functions — messages go directly to our Telegram inbox and are not stored in any database
  • No user accounts, no passwords, no payment processing — there is no sensitive user data stored on our servers
  • The Scheme Finder and RTI Tool process everything in your browser — no data leaves your device

While we take reasonable security precautions, no internet transmission is 100% secure. If you have concerns about security, please contact us through our Contact page.

10. Your Privacy Rights

Since MeraHaq collects minimal personal data, your rights are easy to exercise:

  • Right to access: We hold no personal data about you (beyond anonymous analytics aggregates) unless you contacted us via the contact form.
  • Right to delete: If you used our contact form and want your message deleted, contact us and we will delete it within 7 days.
  • Right to opt out of analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.
  • Right to opt out of personalised ads: Visit Google Ads Settings or NAI Opt-out.
  • Right to manage cookies: Use your browser settings to block or delete cookies at any time.

For Indian users, the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDPA) provides rights over your personal data. Since we collect minimal data, your main rights relate to opting out of analytics and advertising as described above.

11. Do Not Sell My Personal Information

MeraHaq does not sell personal information to any third party — ever. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to outside parties for their marketing or commercial purposes.

Google AdSense serves ads based on your browsing behaviour across the web — this is handled directly between Google and your browser and does not involve MeraHaq selling or transferring any data we have collected. You can opt out of Google's interest-based advertising at Google Ads Settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or new features added to the website. When we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of MeraHaq after any changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through our Contact page.

We respond within 24–48 hours.
