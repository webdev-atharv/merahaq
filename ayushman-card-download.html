⬇️ Download Steps

How to Download Ayushman Card by Mobile Number

This is the fastest method. Your mobile number must be linked to your Aadhaar:

  • 1
    Go to Official PortalOpen beneficiary.nha.gov.in on your phone or computer.
  • 2
    Click "Login / Register"Select "Beneficiary" as login type.
  • 3
    Enter Mobile NumberType your mobile number that is linked to Aadhaar. Click "Verify via OTP".
  • 4
    Enter OTPYou will receive a 6-digit OTP on your mobile. Enter it and click Submit.
  • 5
    Select Your State & SearchSelect your state from the dropdown. Your name and family members will appear.
  • 6
    Click "Download Card"Click the download button next to your name. Your Ayushman card PDF will be saved.
📱 Save the PDF After downloading, save the PDF on your phone. You can show this digital card at any empanelled hospital — no need to print it unless the hospital specifically asks.
📄 Download by Aadhaar

How to Download Using Aadhaar Number

If your mobile is not linked to Aadhaar, use this method:

  • 1
    Go to beneficiary.nha.gov.inOpen the portal on your device.
  • 2
    Select "Login with Aadhaar OTP"Choose this option on the login screen.
  • 3
    Enter 12-Digit Aadhaar NumberType your Aadhaar number carefully.
  • 4
    Enter OTP from Aadhaar-Linked MobileOTP will come on the mobile registered with UIDAI/Aadhaar.
  • 5
    Download CardYour beneficiary profile will appear. Click Download Card to save the PDF.
🏢 No Internet? Visit CSC Center: If you cannot download online, visit your nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or Ayushman Mitra. They will download and print the card for you. Charge is usually ₹0–₹30.
✔️ Eligibility

How to Check if You Are Eligible for Ayushman

Not everyone is eligible. Check before downloading:

  • 1
    Go to pmjay.gov.inClick "Am I Eligible" on the homepage.
  • 2
    Enter Mobile NumberEnter your mobile number and captcha. Click Generate OTP.
  • 3
    Select State & Search by Name/HHDSearch by your name, HHD number, or ration card number.
  • 4
    Check ResultIf your family is listed — you are eligible. Proceed to download your card.

🏥 Coverage

What Does Ayushman Card Cover?

Your Ayushman Bharat card covers a wide range of treatments — completely free at empanelled hospitals:

  • Surgeries: Heart surgery, kidney transplant, brain surgery, cancer surgery and 1,900+ other procedures
  • Hospitalisation: Room charges, nursing, ICU, ventilator — all covered
  • Medicines: All medicines during hospital stay are free
  • Diagnostics: X-ray, MRI, CT scan, blood tests during treatment — free
  • Pre-hospitalisation: 3 days before admission expenses covered
  • Post-hospitalisation: 15 days after discharge expenses covered
  • Day care treatments: 1,400+ treatments that do not need overnight stay
💰 ₹5 Lakh Per Family Per Year: The ₹5 lakh limit resets every year on April 1. It covers the entire family — not per person. If one family member uses ₹3 lakh, the remaining ₹2 lakh is available for others.
